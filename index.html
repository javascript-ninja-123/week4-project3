Each original piece of furniture is unique, and made from a single piece of wood

We combine quality solid wood from exotic trees with other materials such as stone, glass and metal to produce an aesthetic unit that will, thanks to its permanent artistic value, dominate any interior. In every piece you can feel our passion for the work we do. Traditional methods and handmade production guarantee the unique quality of each table.

- ABOUT US

These are tables that will imbue your home with the bountiful spirit of nature

When creating our products we are inspired by feelings coming out from respect and admiration of creations of nature. We produce tables which tell you their past and confess all their „wounds” and edges. They bring perfection of nature to your home in its authetic form.

Always unique, always made from a single piece of wood. Always original furniture. Forget regular shapes... our furniture is a symphony of naturalness and original handwriting of nature. Feel the pure touch, rich colours and pleasent warmth...
